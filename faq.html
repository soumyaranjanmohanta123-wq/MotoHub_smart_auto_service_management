Shipping & Delivery

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days depending on your location. Expedited shipping options (2-day and Overnight) are available at checkout. Please note that custom or built-to-order parts may require additional lead time as stated on the product page.
Yes, we ship to most major countries across North America, Europe, and Asia. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Please be aware that you may be responsible for customs/duty fees upon arrival.
Once your order ships, you will receive an email containing the tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also view your tracking information by logging into your MOTOHUB account and viewing your order history.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns on most unused, uninstalled items in their original packaging within 30 days of receipt. Please see our full Returns Policy for details and exclusions (such as electronics and custom orders).
To start a return, please log in to your account, navigate to your Orders, and select "Request Return." We will review your request and provide you with an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number and shipping instructions.

Products & Compatibility

Our website features a comprehensive "Garage" tool. By entering your vehicle's Year, Make, Model, and Trim/Engine, our system will automatically filter the catalog to only show guaranteed compatible parts. If you are unsure, you can always contact our tech support team.
It depends. Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act in the US, a dealer cannot void your entire vehicle's warranty simply because you installed an aftermarket part. They must prove that the aftermarket part caused the failure. However, aggressive modifications (like ECU tunes or heavily modified forced induction) carry a higher risk.
